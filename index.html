SECURITY SERVICES

About Us

Pride Africa is one of the few South African based black economic empowerment security companies that specialize in special events, corporate and general security services. This is because we are committed in protection, order, safety and security as well as to the preservation of life, dignity and property of all clients and citizens.

Our approach combines professional training, state-of-the-art technology, and a deep understanding of the local security landscape to deliver superior protection. We strive to be a reliable and trustworthy partner in securing your assets and personnel.

Mission

To create a secure and safe environment for our clients and the general public, and to become the leading security services provider in South Africa and beyond.

Vision

To be the preferred supplier of integrated security solutions through value-added services, technology, and superior customer satisfaction.

Objectives

  • Provide high-quality security services.
  • Ensure 100% customer satisfaction.
  • Promote continuous employee development.
  • Contribute positively to community safety.
